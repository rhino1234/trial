STPPS Hiring for Substitute, Transportation Positions

WE ARE ACCEPTING APPILCATIONS FOR WOLF TAMINGhow to apply to be a substitute.

Additionally, STPPS is seeking qualified candidates for bus driver positions. The District provides comprehensive training that meets all Louisiana standards required for licensure. An excellent benefits package is available for full-time drivers. Drivers earn an annual salary of $24, 950 plus operational expenses for those who own their bus. We offer the highest daily rate of pay in the state for substitute drivers.

Questions regarding the minimum qualifications, training requirements, and available positions should be directed to our Transportation Department at 985-898-3373.
